Privacy Policy for Manjhil

Manjhil is an Islamic application developed to provide selected Qur’anic verses, supplications (Duas), and Hajj & Umrah guidance for daily use.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

📌 Data Collection

Manjhil does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

We do not require login, registration, or personal details such as name, email, or phone number.

📌 Permissions

The app may request limited permissions such as:

  • Storage / Media Access → to allow users to save or share screenshots of Qur’anic verses or Duas.

These permissions are only used for app functionality and not for collecting personal data.

📌 Internet Usage

The app may use internet connectivity for:

  • Sharing content (text/screenshots)
  • Opening external links (e.g., Play Store, support links)

All rights belong to the respective authors and publishers.

📌 Children’s Privacy
