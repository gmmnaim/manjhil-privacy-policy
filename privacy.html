Privacy Policy for Manjhil

Manjhil is an Islamic application developed to provide selected Qur’anic verses and supplications (Duas) for daily Ruqyah practice.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Collection

Manjhil does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Permissions

The app may request storage access only for sharing screenshots of Qur’anic verses or Duas.

Internet Usage

The application may use internet connectivity for sharing content.

Children’s Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
